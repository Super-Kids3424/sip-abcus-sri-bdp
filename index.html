Consistent progress requires systemic collaboration. Please review your parent toolkit requirements:

Punctuality

Classes operate tightly for 2 hours and 15 minutes. Always arrive on time to maximize brain warmup.

Attendance

Missed a class? Center team will immediately arrange a compulsory catch-up class for thusday.

Materials

Ensure your child puts SIP uniform, carries SIP books and the abacus kit to every Class.

Daily Practice

Log 10-15 minutes of daily practice at home. It will focus the child memory.
